# Color codes

## Colour Codes

* `&0` - Black
* `&1` - Dark Blue
* `&2` - Dark Green
* `&3` - Dark Aqua
* `&4` - Dark Red
* `&5` - Dark Purple
* `&6` - Gold
* `&7` - Gray
* `&8` - Dark Gray
* `&9` - Blue
* `&A` - Green
* `&B` - Aqua
* `&C` - Red
* `&D` - Light Purple
* `&E` - Yellow
* `&F` - White

## Formatting Codes

* `&M` - Strikethrough
* `&N` - Underline
* `&L` - Bold
* `&K` - Random
* `&0` - Italic
