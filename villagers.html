Villagers



Vanilla+ Specific Changes


    When villagers are placed infront of a workstation, like they would be in a trading hall, their AI becomes disabled to save server resources.
      As normal, the villagers will restock twice per Minecraft day.
      When the villager trade menu is opened, the villager will check if it is next to a workstation, if it is, its AI will be disabled
        Similarly, in the oposite way, when the villager trade menu is opened, the villager will check if it is no longer next to a workstation, and if so re-enable AI
        Once this has happend, normal villager mechanics kick in and if the villager is no longer connected to a workstation, it will lose its profession.
